Frequently Asked Questions

 

  • What is Micro-learning?

    Micro-learning is an engaging and effective teaching method used to train adults and adolescents alike in an era of short attention spans and constant distraction. At EduTutor we break mathematical concepts down into topical, bite-sized chunks to promote student participation and higher retention rates.

  • How is EduTutor different from other Tutoring Companies?

    Large international tutoring companies are expensive, generic, and designed for in-person lessons. EduTutor is a small organization created especially to help students prepare for Ontario's Grade 9 Math Curriculum during a time of unprecedented academic disruption at affordable prices.

  • How does an EduTutor Math Session work?

    With EduTutor an experienced High School Math Teacher breaks topics down into easy to digest micro-learning sessions. Students are then divided into small groups and assigned virtual 'break-out rooms, where a Math tutor works with them directly. Students are encouraged to ask questions and collaborate with each other to complete problem sheets.
